# Ingredients
1 1/2 cups tomatoes
1 fresh pepper
1/4 cup sweet pepper
1/4 cup green onion
3-4 Tsp cilantro
2 Tsp lime juice
1-2 jalapenos 
1 clove garlic

# Directions

Chop every thing up and toss in a bowl. Mix well.
